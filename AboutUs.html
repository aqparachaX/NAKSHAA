About Us

Hey, I'm Abdul Qayyum, a 17-year-old student and the founder of NAKSHA.

I’m currently in college, juggling classes during the day and working on NAKSHA by night. It’s not easy — but building something meaningful never is. What started as a side project soon turned into a vision: to create clean, minimal, and wearable designs that resonate with youth around the world.

“I don’t have a team or a fancy budget — I just have passion, persistence, and a purpose.”

NAKSHA reflects my journey as a young entrepreneur — raw, real, and relentless. Each product is carefully selected, each design thoughtfully curated. I believe in creating something original, something that people like me would actually wear.

Whether you're here to shop or just see what I’m building — thank you for being part of it. Your support means more than you know.

— Abdul Qayyum, Founder of NAKSHA
